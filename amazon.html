January 28, 2018
Color: ClearVerified Purchase
It's slim, it works well, and if you're not stupid, it is enough. I had one of these on my iPhones 5s and 6s over the past few years, without any problem. It looks like there is no case, but it offers basic protections against drops. In my opinion, it is the best looking no-nonsense cover.
For my iPhone X, I chose this case with a separate glass screen cover, like I had done with my older iPhones. The next day, I went to Home Depot and picked up and cradled a post beam weighing around 50 lbs with my Xphone in my pocket. Later, I noticed a pretty good crack in the glass screen cover after the beam had pushed on my breast pocket. After I replaced the screen cover, there's a very small crack in the bottom corner of the glass of the iPhone, which is hard to see, but it's there. Because I am careless, I moved my phone into another clear case that has more edge support for peace of mind.
The new all glass iPhone X is pretty tough, before I got the case my Xphone dropped out of my pocket onto smooth PCC concrete at a gas station and just bounced, undamaged. Nevertheless, it seems to me the edge is vulnerable in torsion, when some of the material comes into tension rather than compression, so if you are doing physical work while carrying your Xphone, this very slim case might not offer enough protection. This cover is fine for the iPhone X, as long as its user is aware of this limitation.
